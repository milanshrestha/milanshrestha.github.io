In today's competitive hospitality landscape, a powerful Property Management System (PMS) is the heart of any hotel's operations. However, its true potential is only unlocked when it is seamlessly integrated with other crucial hotel systems. From point-of-sale (POS) systems to in-room entertainment and guest-facing mobile apps, a well-integrated technology stack can create a frictionless experience for both staff and guests.

This guide explores the key benefits of PMS integration, including centralized data management, automated workflows, and personalized guest services. We'll delve into the most common integration points and provide a roadmap for hoteliers looking to build a connected and efficient technology ecosystem.
